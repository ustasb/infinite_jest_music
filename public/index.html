Infinite Jest (composer Kevin Binder and digitalist Tristan Swedish) are an electronic group that specialize in live performance.The duo merge live improvisation, mind-bending visuals, and a sound-scape that ranges from outlandish to modish, in order to deliver the ultimate sensory experience. Infinite Jest are based in Boston, MA.
